# Mega-Store Sales and Profit Analysis (MS Excel)
Excel report analysing Sales, Profit and regional performance

## 📌 Project Overview

This is a multi-page interactive Excel report designed to provide both high-level insights and detailed analysis for business decision-making.

This project analyzes sales and profit data of a retail store to identify trends, top-performing categories, and areas needing improvement.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to monitor sales performance across regions, categories, and customer segments.

This report helps identify key performance drivers and problem areas to support better decision-making.

  ## 💡 Business Recommendations
  
- Consumer Segment = High Sales, Low Margin
 Improve profitability via better pricing and reduced discounts
  Introduce bundling and upselling strategies
- Technology = Top Revenue & Profit Driver
  Invest more in Technology, inventory, marketing, upselling
  Expand premium Product lines
- West Region Leads Performance
  Evaluate regional demand gaps and adjust product assortment
  Replicate its strategy in weaker regions like Central.
- Sales Concentrated in Few States
  Expand into mid-performing states; optimize or rethink low-performing ones.
- Certain Cities Have Low Profitability
  Review costs, pricing, and discounting in those areas.
- Shipping Costs Affect Margins
  Promote Standard shipping and optimize premium shipping pricing.
